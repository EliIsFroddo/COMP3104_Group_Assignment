Importance of learning DevOps
Higher Speed and Quality of Product Releases : DevOps speeds up product release by introducing continuous delivery, encourage faster feedback, and allowing developers to fix bugs in the system in the early stages
Faster Resposiveness to Customer Needs : With DevOps, a team can react to change requests from customers more efficiently. 
Better Working Environment : Devops principles and practices leads to better communication between teams. 
Optimzes the Entire Business : The biggest advantage of DevOps is the insight it provides
